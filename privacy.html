Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Effective immediately

Tonight ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Tonight — Events & Nightlife mobile application and website. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, phone number) when you sign up
  • Profile details (city, event preferences) you choose to add
  • Messages you send to promoters or venues through the app

We also collect information automatically when you use the app:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Pages and events you view, searches you perform
  • Approximate location (city level) if you grant permission, for showing local events
  • Push notification tokens, if you opt in to notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Tonight service
  • To send you event reminders and alerts you subscribed to
  • To show you relevant events near your location
  • To communicate service updates, security notices, and support responses
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Sharing of Information

We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Promoters & Venues: When you purchase a ticket or RSVP, your name and contact are shared with the organiser to fulfill the booking.
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate (hosting, analytics, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect the safety of users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. Anonymised analytics data may be retained longer.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your country, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Withdraw location permission via your device settings

6. Cookies & Analytics

The Tonight web app uses minimal analytics to understand how the service is used (page views, session counts). No advertising cookies or cross-site tracking are used. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without losing core functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Tonight is intended for users 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit and store passwords in hashed form. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notice or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
